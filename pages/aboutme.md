I am currently a master student at Qutech(TU Delft) in the Borregaard Group, supported by TU Delft Excellence Scholarship. Before I came to Delft, I obtained my bachelor degree in engineering mechanics at Tsinghua University in 2020. Zheng is my family name.

I have a broad research interest including Quantum Network, Quantum Simulation, Quantun Algorithm and Quantum Information. Specifically, my thesis research involves designing and modeling entanglement distribution protocols for the realistic quantum network. I am now making effort to materialize my current work into a published form.

I love reading interesting theoretical papers. You can check my irregular paper digests. Apart from quantum relevant research, I am also interested in physics models in social science(a.k.a Social Physics) and physics description in daily objects. For example, I love the [birdnest PRL paper](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.128.198003) and [the ranking paper in Nat. Comm.](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-29256-x).

Regarding coding, I am familiar with Qiskit and Pennylane. 

I can speak Japanese in a daily level.
